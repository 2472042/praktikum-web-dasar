About Bikini Bottom

Bikini bottom is the vibrant underwater city where Spongebob Squarepants lives. It is home to the Krusty Krab, Jellyfish Fields, and the Chum Bucket.

Top Attractions

-Krusty Krab: Home of the famous Krabby Patty, Don't forget to try one!

-Jellyfish FIelds: Perfect for jellyfish catching and picnics.

-Goo Lagoon: A beatiful lagoon for swimming and sunbathing.

-Krusty Krab: Visit Sandy Cheeks freedom for a taste of Texas underwater.

Travel Tips

- Bring your fellyfishing net for fun day at Jellyfields.

- Don't forget to try a Krabby Party at the Krusty Krab.

- Becareful around Plankton's Chum Bucket-it's not as tasty as it sounds!

About the Author

Spongebob SquarePants is a fry cook at the Krusty Crab and a proud resident of Bikini Bottom. He loves jellyfishing, bubble blowing, and sharing his adventures with the world.
